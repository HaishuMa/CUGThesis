\cugabstract{随着人们物质生活水平的提高和市场竞争的日益激烈，产品的质量和功能也向更高的水平发展，产品的制造过程也越来越复杂，为了满足高质量、高产、低耗，以及安全生产、环保的要求，而过程控制作为工业自动化重要分支，其任务也越来越繁重，我们对于过程控制的发展也越来越重视。本文回顾了过程控制系统的发展历程，结合现在的进程并对其以后的发展作出展望。}{With the improvement of people's material standard of living and increasingly fierce market competition, product quality and function also to a higher level of development, product manufacturing process is more and more complex, in order to meet the requirements of high quality, high yield, low consumption, as well as safe production, environmental protection, and process control as an important branch of industrial automation, its task is more and more heavy, We also pay more and more attention to the development of process control. This paper reviews the development history of process control system, combines the present process and makes prospects for its future development.}
